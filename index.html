Preserving Ethiopian Craft Traditions

Hanos Engraving began as a small family workshop in Addis Ababa, where master artisans passed down the ancient techniques of Ethiopian leatherwork and wood carving. Today, we continue this legacy, combining traditional methods with contemporary designs to create timeless pieces that honor our heritage.

Our craftsmen come from generations of artisans who have perfected the intricate patterns and motifs unique to Ethiopian culture. Each piece tells a story, connecting you to the rich history and vibrant spirit of Ethiopia.

Our Journey Through Time

2020 - Humble Beginnings

Started in a small workshop in Addis Ababa, crafting custom leather journals and carved wooden crosses rooted in Ethiopian tradition.

Workshop Image

2021 - Local Recognition

Featured at Addis Ababa's Artisan Expo. Expanded operations with a growing team of skilled engravers and woodworkers, blending heritage with innovation.

Award Image

2022 - Digital Expansion

Launched the Hanos Engraving Instagram page to showcase personalized gifts and home décor. Orders began reaching customers across Ethiopia and the diaspora.

Global Image

Present Day - Legacy

Hanos Engraving is now preparing to expand globally—bringing Ethiopian craftsmanship to the world through custom wood and leather engraving.
